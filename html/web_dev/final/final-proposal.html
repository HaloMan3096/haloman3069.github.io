Final Proposal

The final project is a 'Fictitious' truth. The 'Truth' I decided to go with was that the earth isn't circular, but it is a cube. I enjoy this Fictitious truth since a common conspiracy theory is that the earth is flat and i want to poke fun at that idea. I want to make it in the same vain as 'The Book of Mormon' musical and how it pokes fun while still being somewhat respectful

The organization that is presenting this info is "The Cube Earth Society" and after looking up "the cube earth society" I found a funny picture where the slogan is "Because a Flat Earth is just stupid" and that just cracks me up. This organization would present photos from Minecraft as their "proof" that the earth is a cube and not a sphere or flat.

This could definitely end up being useful since it is purposely fictitious it could help people look into it and help reinforce the idea of a sphere earth. Though a lot of people tend to read stuff and not double-check the statements and that could cause issues though it should be obvious that the cube earth society isn't real.
